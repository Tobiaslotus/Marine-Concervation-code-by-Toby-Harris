This website will help you to understand what marine concervation is and why people pollute along with why people enact marine concervation and how you can help with marine concervation.













































Marine Conservation is also known as Ocean conservation: which is the health of sea creatures and sea fauna. People all over the world became aware of the state that the sea creatures and plants were in in the 19 60s, around this time there was a person named Jacques Cousteau who brought the wonders of ocean creatures and marine life to television.

In the 1917s the U.S made laws which were enforced to protect marine life such as a law to stop pollution and a law to carfully protect sea creatures who were endangered species but there are still some issues to this day. For example: acidification, climate change, sea level rising, decreasing bycatch in fisheries and establishing protected areas for endangered species.

There are three types of marine protection: Marine Reserves, Marine Sanctuarys and Marine National Parks and Refuges. The next page will help you to understand why and how people pollute the ocean and cause marine life to suffer.
